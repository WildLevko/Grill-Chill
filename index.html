Cheeseburger🍔🧀

This is a juicy, mouth-watering burger on a soft bun, sandwiched between two halves of perfectly seared beef patties, melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers and onions. All this is generously seasoned with sauces such as mayonnaise or ketchup, which gives the dish a unique juiciness and taste. The taste of the combination of a hot cutlet and melting cheese becomes a real gastronomic pleasure for every fast food lover.

Chicken-Burger🍔🍗

This is a delicious burger with a tender chicken cutlet fried until golden brown. Inside a soft bun, juicy chicken is perfectly paired with fresh vegetables - crisp lettuce, tomato and cucumber slices. To this are added soft cheeses and savory sauces that highlight the delicate taste of the chicken.

Veggie-Burger🍔🥬

This is a healthy and delicious burger that uses vegetable patties as a base, made from fresh ingredients such as chickpeas, beans, carrots and spices. The soft bun wraps not only around the patty, but also around fresh vegetables—lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and avocados—creating a vibrant palette of flavors and textures. Dips like hummus or yogurt sauce add creaminess and lightness.
